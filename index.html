DEGEN Shuffles

Fair shuffles for $DEGEN holders

DEGEN Shuffles is a tool to pick random wallets of $degen holders. Each shuffle is an ASA created on the Algorand blockchain. These ASAs are used to store shuffle configurations and keep an history of the winners over time.

The odds of a wallet getting picked depend on the amount of $degen it holds, and decrease with each win.
